I am a young, creatively ambitious writer looking to collaborate with a team in the creation of something fantastic.

My name is Ricardo Perez.

I started writing at a young age in a journal where I recorded thoughts, reflections and insights of the world around and within. This was shortly before starting my degree in psychology.

During my studies at the university I kept a dream journal, did research on various psychology paradigms (old and new) and focused on psychoanalysis. Specifically, a volume of Sigmund Freud’s first works that I still own.

More recently, I became acquainted with other forms of literature, specifically classic novels of different regions and science fiction. I created a blog in which I published short stories parallel to the lore of a infamous videogame.

Now I’m finishing a postgraduate degree on Scriptwriting at Bournemouth University.
